Season 1: The Grounding Chronicles

Join GhostVista on his most challenging adventure yet - surviving being grounded! What starts as a simple punishment quickly spirals into a series of extraordinary events when he discovers a mysterious portal in his room. This season follows GhostVista as he navigates the ups and downs of being grounded while uncovering secrets that will change his life forever. From making unexpected friends to facing his greatest fears, every episode is packed with humor, heart, and just the right amount of chaos.
